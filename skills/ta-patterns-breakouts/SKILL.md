# TA Patterns And Breakouts

## Overview

Summarize high-probability A-share chart patterns and breakout confirmation rules.

## Reversal Patterns

- Head-and-shoulders: confirm with volume fade on right shoulder and breakout volume.
- Double/Triple tops and bottoms: require close confirmation and >=3% breakout filter.
- Rounded bottom and V-reversal: treat V as event-driven, use staged entry.

## Continuation Patterns

- Triangles: expect breakout in trend direction; confirm on close and volume.
- Flags and wedges: require prior "flagpole" move and volume contraction in the flag.
- Rectangles and channels: buy near support and sell near resistance; treat breaks as trend shifts.

## Gaps

- Ignore common gaps or expect fast fills.
- Treat breakaway gaps with volume as valid; quick fills imply failure.
- Treat exhaustion gaps as reversal warnings and cut risk immediately.

## Breakout And Pullback Rules

- Breakout confirmation: close >=3% beyond boundary with volume expansion.
- Pullback entry: 38.2%-61.8% retrace, 3-5 days, and stabilization signal.
- Failure rules: fast re-entry into pattern or heavy sell volume on pullback.

## Targets And Risk

- Use pattern height for target projection.
- Place stop just beyond the broken boundary or neckline.
